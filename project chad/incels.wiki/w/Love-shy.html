Love-shy.net is the forum that started after love-shy.com closed. Love-shy.com which was the longest lasting forum in the incelosphere.

Birth[edit | edit source]

Mr. C closed down love-shy.com without transferring the database or providing a redirect to love-shy.net. A moderator and 'resident historian' from love-shy.com named Ieatboogers created and owns love-shy.net
