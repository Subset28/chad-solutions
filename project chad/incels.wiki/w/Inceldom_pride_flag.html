The incel flag.

The inceldom flag or inceldom pride flag is meant to represent involuntary celibate people. In order to forge a group identity, some incelists and ardent members of the incelosphere have attempted to create an incel flag in order to forge a group identity and to increase the visibility of nearcels, truecels and incels within wider society.

Meaning[edit | edit source]

The various shades of grey denote the inceldom spectrum with each outward vertical bar representing an escalating degree of the incel status. The white bar at the center represents functional members of society (noncels, normies or Chads who are effective at courtship and dating). The lightest shades represent the nearcels, incels and reluctant volcels. The darkest shades of grey represents the ultimate incels, truecels.

Alternative flag[edit | edit source]

The incel pride flag, as designed by Sans.

An alternative flag was designed by incels.co user Sans. The colors represent the three main pills, and the flag represents the transition from bluepilled to redpilled to blackpilled that incels go through. This flag has has been noted to look like the flag of Polyamory, however, the polyamorous flag has a yellow pi symbol in the middle, while this flag does not.

Blackpilled incels flag[edit | edit source]

[Image missing]

On a poll held from 10/20/20 to 10/22/20 incels.co users chose this flag over 7 others. The flag was created around 2018 by an unknown artist on r/braincels. Each color represents one of the three main pills, and the black in particular is "marching" forward overtaking the red and blue pills as it represents the truth. Additionally, the flag is not used to represent all incels, it is exclusively used to recognize and unify blackpilled incels.
