Rename to manosphere.wiki[edit source]

User:Eth among others others have proposed to rename the wiki to a less prejudiced name like manosphere.wiki, especially since the topics covered here have long grown past more incel topics and memes.

I've initially opposed this idea for a number of reasons.

But I now think the main pro argument is that the mainstream media will presumably never rehabilitate the incel label and keep associate it with a minority of Category:bad people who have referenced incel memes. I was hopeful a while ago, but I haven't seen any tendency for improvement since. Even though we have lots of good material here, it may not have majority appeal due to the incel label. Thoughts? Bibipi (talk) 10:25, 27 February 2020 (UTC)
